    A Taste of Go

    A Taste of Go

    7 Sep 2022

    Go feels like a modernist language: its syntactic simplicity leaves no room for human-friendly affordances that make a language a true joy to use. Like some modernist architecture, Go consists of a few, simple elements neatly packaged together and features best-in-class aspects like its generational garbage collector and lightweight goroutines. Despite these and other aspects that make Go a huge improvement on unsafe or heavyweight languages like C and Java, it lacks some of the spice that makes it a nice language to use.
